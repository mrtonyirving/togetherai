## How EU law relates to national enforcement

When a national authority (e.g., FI in Sweden) takes enforcement action, the cited national regulation typically implements an EU directive or regulation. The `IMPLEMENTS` relation type captures this chain, enabling navigation from a Swedish enforcement action all the way to the underlying EU instrument.
