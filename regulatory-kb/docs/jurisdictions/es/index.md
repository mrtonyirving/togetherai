# Spain

## Regulatory authorities

Spain has three financial supervisory authorities:

### CNMV (Comisión Nacional del Mercado de Valores)
- **Role**: Securities markets, investment firms, fund managers
- **Website**: [https://www.cnmv.es](https://www.cnmv.es)

### Banco de España (BdE)
- **Role**: Banks, credit institutions, payment services
- **Website**: [https://www.bde.es](https://www.bde.es)

### DGSFP (Dirección General de Seguros y Fondos de Pensiones)
- **Role**: Insurance companies, pension funds
- **Website**: [https://www.dgsfp.mineco.es](https://www.dgsfp.mineco.es)

## Legal framework

Spain is an EU member. EU directives are transposed via Leyes (laws), Reales Decretos (royal decrees), and Órdenes Ministeriales (ministerial orders).

## Key legislation

| Law | Area | EU basis |
|---|---|---|
| Ley 10/2010 | AML/CFT | AMLD4 |
| Real Decreto Legislativo 4/2015 | Securities | MiFID II |
| Ley 20/2015 | Insurance solvency | Solvency II |

## Entity identifiers

Spanish entities use a **CIF/NIF** (tax identification number).
