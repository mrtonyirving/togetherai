# Norway

## Regulatory authorities

### Finanstilsynet

- **Role**: Financial supervisory authority for banking, insurance, securities, and pensions.
- **Website**: [https://www.finanstilsynet.no](https://www.finanstilsynet.no)
- **Enforcement actions**: [https://www.finanstilsynet.no/tema/sanksjoner/](https://www.finanstilsynet.no/tema/sanksjoner/)

## Legal framework

Norway is not an EU member but is part of the EEA. EU financial regulation is incorporated into Norwegian law through the EEA Agreement. The chain is: EU Directive/Regulation → EEA Agreement → Norwegian Law/Forskrift.

## Key legislation

| Law | Area | EU basis |
|---|---|---|
| Hvitvaskingsloven (2018) | AML/CFT | AMLD4 |
| Verdipapirhandelloven (2007) | Securities | MiFID II |
| Finansforetaksloven (2015) | Financial institutions | CRD/Solvency II |

## Entity identifiers

Norwegian entities use a 9-digit **organisasjonsnummer** (no hyphen).
