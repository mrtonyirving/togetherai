# Sweden

## Regulatory authorities

### Finansinspektionen (FI)

- **Role**: Financial supervisory authority. Supervises banks, insurance companies, securities firms, fund managers, and other financial institutions.
- **Website**: [https://www.fi.se](https://www.fi.se)
- **Enforcement actions**: [https://www.fi.se/sv/publicerade-dokument/sanktioner/](https://www.fi.se/sv/publicerade-dokument/sanktioner/)
- **Regulations (FFFS)**: [https://www.fi.se/sv/vara-register/forfattningssamling/](https://www.fi.se/sv/vara-register/forfattningssamling/)

## Legal framework

Swedish financial regulation operates on three levels:

1. **Laws (Lagar)** — enacted by the Riksdag (parliament)
2. **Ordinances (Förordningar)** — issued by the government
3. **Regulations (Föreskrifter / FFFS)** — issued by FI

FI's own regulations are published in the FFFS series (Finansinspektionens författningssamling).
