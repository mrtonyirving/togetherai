## Scope

Directives are EU legislative instruments that must be transposed into national law. They are the top of the regulatory chain for most national enforcement actions.

## Key directives tracked

- AMLD4/5/6 (Anti-Money Laundering)
- MiFID II (Markets in Financial Instruments)
- Solvency II (Insurance)
- CRD IV/V (Capital Requirements)
- BRRD (Bank Recovery and Resolution)
- PSD2 (Payment Services)
- IDD (Insurance Distribution)
