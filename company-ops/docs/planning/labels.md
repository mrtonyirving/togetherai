# Labels and Meanings

Apply a single taxonomy to keep triage and reporting consistent.

## Type Labels

- `type:bug` - Defect impacting expected behavior.
- `type:feature` - New product capability.
- `type:task` - Internal task or maintenance work.
- `type:decision` - Decision request requiring documented outcome.
- `type:incident` - Production or operational incident.
- `type:docs` - Documentation-only work.

## Area Labels

- `area:product`
- `area:engineering`
- `area:ops`
- `area:security`
- `area:customers`
- `area:hiring`

## Priority Labels

- `p0` - Immediate action required.
- `p1` - High priority this cycle.
- `p2` - Important but not urgent.
- `p3` - Backlog or exploratory.

## State/Risk Labels

- `blocked` - External/internal dependency blocks progress.
- `needs-info` - Missing data to proceed.
- `needs-decision` - Explicit decision pending.
- `risk:high` - Elevated risk requiring mitigation.
- `good-first-issue` - Suitable onboarding task.
