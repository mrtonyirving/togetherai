# Incident Response Basics

## Severity

- `SEV1`: Critical outage or major security event.
- `SEV2`: Significant degradation with workaround.
- `SEV3`: Localized or low-impact issue.

## Initial Response

1. Open `incident` issue immediately.
2. Assign incident commander.
3. Stabilize customer impact.
4. Preserve evidence and timeline.

## Follow-up

- Publish postmortem within 2 business days.
- Link corrective actions to owners and due dates.
