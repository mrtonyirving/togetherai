# Company Handbook

## Mission and Principles

- Ship value quickly.
- Keep decisions explicit.
- Optimize for clarity over ceremony.

## Working Norms

- Default to async updates in GitHub.
- Use issues for requests and PRs for meaningful changes.
- Document major decisions and incidents within 48 hours.

## Escalation

- Operational incidents: create `incident` issue and runbook follow-up.
- Product direction conflicts: open `decision` issue and RFC/PRD.
